# 05_Backup_Disaster_Recovery.md

## Objective

You are acting as a Principal Enterprise Infrastructure Architect responsible for designing the Backup & Disaster Recovery Specification for an Enterprise School Management System (SMS).

This document defines the enterprise backup architecture, disaster recovery strategy, business continuity planning, restoration processes, recovery objectives, operational resilience, and future high-availability readiness for the complete School Management System.

This document establishes enterprise operational standards that ensure institutional data, services, and business operations remain recoverable after failures while remaining implementation-independent.
