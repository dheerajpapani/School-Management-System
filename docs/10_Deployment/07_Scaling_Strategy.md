# 07_Scaling_Strategy.md

## Objective

You are acting as a Principal Enterprise Infrastructure Architect and Site Reliability Engineer (SRE) responsible for designing the Scaling Strategy Specification for an Enterprise School Management System (SMS).

This document defines the enterprise scaling architecture governing application scalability, infrastructure expansion, database growth, workload distribution, resource optimization, multi-campus readiness, SaaS readiness, and future operational growth.

This document establishes enterprise standards ensuring the School Management System can evolve from a single-school deployment to a nationwide multi-campus or SaaS platform without requiring architectural redesign.

This document remains implementation-independent.
